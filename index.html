About Me

My name is Mary Esso, i am a passionate professional with a unique blend of expertise in health, wellness, and technology. With a strong foundation in Medical Biochemistry and over two years of experience as a Laboratory Scientist, I bring analytical and scientific rigor to all my work. As a Laboratory Scientist, my role involves collection of blood samples, analyzing blood, tissue, and other samples with precision, using sophisticated equipment to detect abnormalities and ensure accurate results. As a Yoga Instructor, I guide individuals on their journey to physical and mental well-being. My practice focuses on creating a balanced environment where students can explore the connection between body and mind through breath and movement. I specialize in customizing sessions to meet the unique needs of each student, whether they seek relaxation, flexibility, or strength. My goal is to cultivate a space of mindfulness and inner peace, helping others to embrace the transformative power of yoga in their daily lives. Additionally, I leverage my skills in Web Development to create dynamic, user-friendly websites, bridging the gap between science, wellness, and technology. So yeah, that’s a brief summary about me😊
